Draws a circle based on Bresenhams circle drawing algorithm.

Draws 1/8th of the circle with centre (x0,y0) and radius r. The rest of it is drawn by circle_points().

Parameters
x0x coordinate of the centre of the circle.
y0y coordinate of the centre of the circle.
rThe radius of the circle.
